Who we are

Founded at the University of Minnesota - Twin Cities, Algora Finance holds dear the principles that we learned while investing early on. We realize the importance of luck, and thus our slogan was born - Making the lucky luckier.

What we do

We live in a day where computers can operate more efficiently and with less bias than a human, especially in regard to data analytics. This is precisely why we are dedicated to build unique algorithms to help construct risk-optimized portfolios that fit the personal preferences of each and every client.
